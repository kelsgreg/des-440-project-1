Nashville Food Truck Fest

HOW WE STARTED

he Nashville Food Truck Fest started in Nashville in 2013. We started with 10 food trucks and it has grown in past years! Last year there were over 80 different food trucks.

WHAT WE DO

The second weekend in September (Saturday and Sunday) of each year. We reach out to as many food trucks as we can. We also set up dining area's for you to sit and relax while you try all sorts of different foods! We have all sorts of different food trucks that take part in this yearly event. With breakfast, lunch, dinner and dessert options!

WHO'S GONNA BE THERE

LOCATION

This year we will be at Riverfront Park in downtown Nashville. What's better than trying all sorts of food and being able to have a view of the river?

LIVE MUSIC

Throughout the weekend there will be a Nashville native bands playing. Giving you a chance to not only get to try a bunch of food but also getting to listen to some of your favorite bands while you do it.
